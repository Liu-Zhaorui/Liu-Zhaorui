智能化乐器创新设计工作坊

Innovative Instrument Design International Workshop

Date: SEP 16 - 20, 2019

Place: Central Conservatory of Music, No.43 Baojia Street, Xicheng District, Beijing

Content: This workshop focus on the traditional musical instrument - Duxianqin, which is popular in southern Guangxi and Vietnam, and explore the innovative design of Chinese traditional instruments using digital technology. The participants' work revolves around the shape design of musical instruments, sound synthesis, sensor data collection, interaction design, and other aspects.

Presented by Central Conservatory of Music & Queen Mary University of London, Media Art Technology
